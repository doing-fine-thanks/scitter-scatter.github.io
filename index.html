Release

by Logan H. G. Davis

This project is an attempt to recreate and convey the anxieties and feelings I have and have had as a person with dyslexia. It's an attempt to convey my personal difficulties with reading across intersubjective boundaries.

Black underlined words will progress you through the piece. If you want to follow a different path, simply reload the page to start again. As a warning, the piece does contain audio.
